FAQs

Can I make a booking?

We are currently taking bookings for groups of 12 guests or more for a 3 hour period, and in our indoor seating area only. The rest of the venue, including the beer garden, has ample space for walk-in guests. Please use the form here for further inquiries or to make a booking.

Can I book for less than 12 people?

As we transition back into our regular style of service, we are no longer taking bookings for groups smaller than 12, though we now have a capacity of 250 so there is usually plenty of space for walk-ins!

Can I book for longer than 3 hours?

Not at this stage, but you are welcome to move into a walk-ins area after your booking if there is space to do so. We kindly ask for no back-to-back bookings and only one booking per group only please! We also ask that guests leave the space promptly when their booking concludes to allow staff to thoroughly sanitise the area.

Can I book in the beer garden?

Currently the beer garden is only available for walk-in groups.

Have you adopted COVID safety measures?

To ensure the venue is as safe as possible for patrons and staff we are taking a number of measures including hand sanitizing stations, guest registration, table distancing, new cleaning procedures as well as digital ordering at table and payment.

Tables will maintain a 1.5m distance from other tables to adhere to the 1 person per 2sqm restriction. Contactless ordering is available via QR code at the table and contact details of every patron must be taken for contact tracing. If you or any of your guests are sick please stay home and contact the venue to cancel your booking as soon as possible. If you have any additional questions, please contact us here.

How do I order and pay?

All ordering and payment can be done via your smartphone while at your table or at the bar. You can either order individually, if you’d like to pay separately, or together using the QR code that will be on your table. For more information on what happens when you arrive for a booking, contact us here.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

If you need to cancel for any reason please contact us here.

Are you hosting brewery tours?

Not currently, but we are working towards being able to host brewery tours again ASAP.

I lost something when I visited the Beer Hall. How can I inquire if it has been found?

People lose things in the Beer Hall all the time! Just fill in the form here and we’ll get back to you if we find it.

Can I bring my dog?

Yes! Friendly pups are more than welcome in the Beer Hall and we’d love it if you brought your furry mates along.

Are children welcome?

Yes! We even have a kids menu! Please keep in mind children and infants (even if they are in a pram) are required by the state government guidelines to be counted towards your booking. We ask all parents to supervise their children at all times and to ensure their child has shoes on at all times. We are unable to provide colouring pencils as we have in the past so please bring along entertainment from home for your child.
